Desert Island Game

Guess what you can bring to the desert island! The host will tell you if your guess is correct.

How to Play

One player secretly knows the rule for what can be brought to the island. All other players guess items one by one.

1. Guessing: Enter an item in the text box and click "Guess."

2. The Host: The player who knows the rule checks the item and then clicks either "Place on Island" (if it's allowed) or "Place in Ocean" (if it's not allowed).

The game continues until the players figure out the rule!
